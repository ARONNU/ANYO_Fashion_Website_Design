WHY IS GREEN THE NEW SWING?

Why Is Green the New Swing?

After gravitating towards neutral hues in 2021, it seems like people are ready to welcome ~brighter~ colors into their wardrobes again. One particular hue that's all over our feeds right now is the color green! The fresh hue—which comes in various shades, from lime, sage, to forest green—is always relaxing on the eyes. After all, it is strongly associated with nature. NGL, it can sometimes be intimidating to wear green, especially when mixing and matching it with other colors. Our tip? You can start by simply adding a green accessory (Think: A baguette bag or a cap) to your OOTD for a subtle pop of color.
